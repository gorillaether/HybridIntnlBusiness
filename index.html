‚ö†Ô∏è Wise Accounts (Reporting Required)

üè¢ Business Structure Optimization

Legal Entity Considerations

üîß Recommended Structure

Single-Member LLC with S-Corp Election

  • LLC Benefits: Liability protection, operational flexibility
  • S-Corp Election: Payroll tax savings on profits above reasonable salary
  • Pass-through taxation: Avoid double taxation complexities
  • Delaware LLC: Consider for banking access and privacy

üìã Tax Strategy Components

  • Foreign Earned Income Exclusion (FEIE): Up to $120,000+ exclusion for 2024
  • Foreign Tax Credit: Credit for taxes paid to foreign governments
  • Reasonable Salary: Pay yourself W-2 wages for S-Corp election
  • Business Deductions: Home office, travel, equipment expenses

‚úÖ Avoid These Structures

  • Foreign corporations (CFC rules)
  • Complex multi-entity setups
  • PFIC investments
  • Foreign partnerships
